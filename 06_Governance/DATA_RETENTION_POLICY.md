# Data Retention Policy

- Raw artifacts: retain only as long as needed for validation, dispute handling or approved intelligence use.
- Contact and lead data: purpose-limited retention with deletion or suppression workflows.
- Normalized entities and claims: retain with provenance, confidence and review state.
- Secrets and authentication material: never store in artifacts, logs or receipts.
- Quarantined content: restricted access and scheduled review/deletion.
- Audit receipts: durable, append-only and protected from general client access.

Retention periods must be approved by data class and jurisdiction before enforcement. This file activates no deletion or storage rule.
